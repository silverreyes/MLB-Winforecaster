### Non-Blocking Observations

1. **`game_logs.game_id` is VARCHAR; `predictions.game_id` is INTEGER** — cast `::INTEGER` applied consistently in 3 places (`_fetch_final_scores`, `reconcile_outcomes`, `get_history`). Safe in practice (MLB gamePks are always numeric), but a schema alignment improvement could eliminate the cast.
2. **`write_game_outcome` stamps all prediction rows for a game_id** (not just `is_latest=TRUE`) — intentional design, documented in db.py. `get_history` deduplicates via `ROW_NUMBER` post_lineup preference.
3. **Pre-Phase-13 prediction rows excluded from history** (`game_id IS NOT NULL` filter) — correct behavior; old rows cannot be reconciled.
4. **`/api/v1/accuracy` route orphaned** — pre-v2.2 design (AccuracyStrip uses hardcoded Brier scores). No v2.2 requirement affected.
5. **DateNavigator "Today" button: client-side ET calculation vs server-side ET** — documented edge case for UTC+ timezone users. Low impact.
